# Source Notes: Как внедрить ИИ в бизнес в 2026 году

Checked date: 2026-06-10

## Source Rules

- Prefer primary research, official reports, regulator pages and reputable media.
- Date unstable facts in the article or use cautious wording.
- Budget examples are editorial calculations and must not be presented as market prices.

## Sources to Recheck Before Publication

- Market/adoption numbers: CNews/Data Fusion, Yandex/Яков и Партнёры, HSE, McKinsey.
- Legal/regulatory: 152-ФЗ references if the section expands beyond high-level caution.
- Tool examples: CRM/model names if specific product capabilities are added.
