# Outline: Сколько стоит внедрение ИИ в компанию

## H1

Сколько стоит внедрение ИИ в компанию: бюджет, статьи расходов и калькулятор

## Promise

Reader should understand in the first 20 seconds:

- AI implementation is not just subscription/API cost.
- Quick budget bands exist for pilot, bot, agent and custom system.
- The right calculation is launch budget plus monthly operating cost.

## Structure

1. Краткое резюме with budget table and formula.
2. Why model/subscription price is not implementation cost.
3. Budget breakdown: design, data, model/API, integrations, UI, security, QA, support.
4. Fast pilot budget and what pilot proves.
5. Production budget and why it costs more.
6. Calculator: launch and monthly costs.
7. Examples: lead qualification, support bot, document processing.
8. Price drivers.
9. Cost reduction.
10. Forgotten expenses.
11. Вывод Malakhov AI.
12. FAQ.
13. Sources.
