# Source Notes: ИИ для производства: сценарии, внедрение и окупаемость

Checked date: 2026-06-19

## Source Rules

- Prefer official docs, product pages, primary research, company reports, regulator pages, and reputable media.
- For unstable facts, write the checked date in the article or use cautious wording.
- Do not invent prices, case studies, claims, product limits, or legal interpretations.

## Sources to Recheck Before Publication

- Product functions: none, unless final article names specific vendor tools.
- Tariffs/pricing: avoid vendor pricing; use worked example as a model calculation.
- Market numbers: Rockwell Automation 2025 survey; verify fieldwork date and respondent scope.
- Legal/regulatory: current text of articles 6 and 11 of 152-ФЗ.
- Russia/CIS context: 1С/MES/ERP examples should be framed as common integration categories, not universal requirements.
