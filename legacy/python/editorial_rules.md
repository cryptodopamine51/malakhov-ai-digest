# Editorial rules — Malakhov AI Digest

## Public sections

### important
Top market-moving AI events of the day.

### ai_news
Models, product releases, research, infrastructure, major big-tech updates.

### coding
Coding models, IDE/CLI tools, agentic coding workflows, open-source tooling, benchmarks.

### investments
Funding rounds, acquisitions, notable startup moves, strategic partnerships.

### alpha
Author-curated layer. For now it is a placeholder section in the product.

## General priorities
1. Official sources > engineering sources > respected media > local media.
2. One event = one future card.
3. Preserve source links.
4. Favor quality over volume.
5. Short summaries should later fit into 2–5 lines.

## Noise rules
- Avoid duplicating the same event across many cards.
- Avoid weak repost-style items when a primary source exists.
- Avoid stuffing thin days with low-value material.

## Section bias guidance
- `important`: strongest market impact, widely relevant.
- `ai_news`: broad AI market and product movement.
- `coding`: tools and workflows valuable to builders.
- `investments`: capital flow and startup momentum.
- `alpha`: manual editorial judgment.
